Sony Inzone H9 Wireless Headphones

Sony

The Inzone H9 are wireless headsets with Bluetooth and 2.4GHz wireless connectivity, with the former preferred for calls and chats with a smartphone, and the latter in use for game audio using a USB dongle. All of the headphones can be customised and used with the Sony Inzone Hub software on PC, for fine tuning of frequency response and equaliser settings. This headphones support Sony's 360 Spartial Sound format for virtualised surround sound. Battery life is claimed to be up to 32 hours. The Inzone H9 also has active noise cancellation.
